Firechip by Celdiven

Firechip is a software program that runs on a PC (Windows®, Mac OS®, GNU/Linux®) to develop applications for Microchip microcontrollers and digital signal controllers.

Dependencies

GTK+ 2.10 or higher GLib 2.12 or higher Pango 1.14 or higher X.Org 1.0 or higher (1.7 or higher is recommended) libstdc++ 4.3 or higher
